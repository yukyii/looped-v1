1Tether — plan & three design directions
Plan & assumptions
Product
Tether — a desktop browser app where friends post open, joinable everyday activities.
Audience
18–35, college students & young professionals. Low-friction, low-pressure social planning.
Signature
Background is a soft pastel gradient keyed to time of day — morning, sunset, night. Every direction below treats this as the canvas the whole UI sits on.
Scope
Home feed → post an activity → notifications → profile & friends → onboarding.
Process
① Pick a direction below (or mix) → ② I build the full design system → ③ full interactive prototype of each flow.
Shared UX principles
  • An activity post is one glanceable line: who · what · where · when, plus who's in.
  • Joining is one click ("I'm in") — no RSVP forms, no pressure states.
  • Time is the organizing axis of the feed, matching the time-of-day background.
  • Posting is a one-line composer at the top of the feed, not a separate page.
